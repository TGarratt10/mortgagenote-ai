FAQ

Common questions

Is this actually FCA compliant? +
MortgageNote AI generates notes that meet MCOB 4 and 11 requirements and address all Consumer Duty obligations. As the regulated adviser, you remain responsible for reviewing and signing off every note before use.
Where is client data stored? +
Nowhere. Client data entered is processed in real time and never stored on our servers. GDPR compliant by design.
Can I edit the notes before filing? +
Yes — and you should. Copy the output, paste into your CRM or Word document, and amend as needed.
What's in the free plan? +
5 complete suitability notes per month — enough to test the tool properly with real clients. No credit card required.
How do I cancel? +
Cancel any time from your account settings. No notice period, no cancellation fee.

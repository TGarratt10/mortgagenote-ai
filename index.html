FAQ

Common questions

Is this actually FCA compliant? +
MortgageNote AI generates notes that meet MCOB 4 and 11 requirements and address all Consumer Duty obligations. However, as the regulated adviser, you remain responsible for reviewing and signing off every note before use. The tool assists your documentation process — it does not replace your professional judgement.
Where is client data stored? +
Nowhere. Client data entered into MortgageNote AI is processed in real time to generate your note and is never stored on our servers. We are GDPR compliant by design. You should still follow your own firm's data handling policies when copying notes to your CRM.
Can I edit the notes before filing them? +
Yes — and you should. The AI generates a strong first draft but you should always review and edit before filing. Copy the output, paste it into your CRM or Word document, and amend anything specific to your client's situation that you want to add.
What's included in the free plan? +
The free plan gives you 5 complete suitability notes per month — enough to test the tool properly with real clients before committing. No credit card required to sign up.
How do I cancel? +
Cancel any time from your account settings. No notice period, no cancellation fee. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
